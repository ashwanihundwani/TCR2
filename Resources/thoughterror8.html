Over-simplifying


When you see one bad event as a pattern that never changes.


Example: “My last date went terribly, I must not be very good company.”


Tinnitus Example: “I was awake all night because of my tinnitus. My tinnitus will keep me awake every night.”


Corrected Thought: “Last night my tinnitus kept me awake, but most nights I eventually fall asleep. I’m learning ways to manage my sleep by using sound and relaxation techniques.”
