Jumping to Conclusions


Coming to believe something is true with no facts to support it. You might assume that you know what someone else is thinking (“Mindreading”) or assume things will turn out badly (“Fortune Telling”).


Example: “If I go to the party where I don’t know anyone, I will not have fun.”


Tinnitus Example: “If my tinnitus is loud when I wake up in the morning I know I will have a bad day.”


Corrected Thought: “I’m learning ways to have a good day even when my tinnitus is loud.”
