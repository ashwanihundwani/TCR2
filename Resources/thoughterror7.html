Making Things Personal

You see yourself as the cause of some negative event when you are not responsible.


Example: “My doctor was not nice to me because I was sick.”


Tinnitus Example: “Because my tinnitus made it hard for me to enjoy the picnic, I caused everyone else to have a bad time.”


Corrected Thought: “My tinnitus made it hard for me to enjoy the picnic but others seemed to have a good time.”
