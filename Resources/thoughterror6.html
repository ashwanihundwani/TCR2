Labeling


Attaching a bad label to yourself or others.


Example: “I have lost my keys again, I’m so stupid.”


Tinnitus Example: “I can’t deal with my tinnitus so I must be a weak person.”


Corrected Thought: “I practice methods for coping with my tinnitus. They help, but my tinnitus still bothers me sometimes. That’s normal; it doesn’t mean I’m weak.”
