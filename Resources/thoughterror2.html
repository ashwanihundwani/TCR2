Blaming


You blame others for your problems. You may also blame yourself for other people’s problems.


Example: “I didn’t get the job because you didn’t call to give me a pep-talk before my meeting.”


Tinnitus Example: “My tinnitus wouldn’t be a problem if my partner were more supportive.”


Corrected Thought: “It would be helpful if my partner were more supportive. Either way I have to manage tinnitus myself.”
