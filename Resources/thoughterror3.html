Emotional Thoughts


When you think that your emotions show the way things really are. You might think, “I feel it, so it must be true.”


Example: “I feel guilty so I must have done something bad.”


Tinnitus Example: “I feel overwhelmed and hopeless about my tinnitus. My problems must be impossible to solve.”


Corrected Thought: “Just because I feel overwhelmed doesn’t mean that my problems can’t be solved.”
